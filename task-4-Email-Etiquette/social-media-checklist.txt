Responsible Social Media Use & Digital Communication – Checklist for College Students

 Do’s
-Think before posting or sending messages to ensure your communication is respectful and appropriate.
-Protect your privacy online by adjusting privacy settings and avoiding sharing personal information.
-Use social media and digital platforms for learning and collaboration with classmates and teachers.
-Maintain polite and professional digital communication in emails, comments, and messages.
-Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.

 Don’ts
-Don’t share sensitive personal details such as passwords, address, or phone numbers online.
-Don’t post offensive, harmful, or inappropriate content that may damage your reputation.
-Don’t engage in cyberbullying or negative online arguments.
-Don’t forward fake news or unverified information.
-Don’t misuse digital communication platforms by sending spam messages or disrespectful comments.
