Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

Tool Used:
For this project, I used Canva to design my infographic. I selected Canva because it provides a variety of easy-to-use templates, icons, and design elements that help in creating visually attractive educational content. It allowed me to organize information clearly while also adding relevant icons and simple graphics. Using Canva made it easier to present important information in a way that is both engaging and easy to understand for students.

Design Overview:
My infographic is organized into three main sections to explain the key aspects of digital literacy. The first section explains what Digital Literacy is, describing it as the ability to use digital technology effectively, responsibly, and safely. The second section focuses on Useful Digital Tools for Students, highlighting platforms such as Google Docs for collaboration, Canva for design, Miro for brainstorming, and online learning platforms like Coursera and Khan Academy. The third section emphasizes Safe Internet Practices, where I included tips such as using strong passwords, avoiding suspicious links, and protecting personal information online.

Reflection:
While working on this infographic, I realized that digital literacy is an essential skill for students in today’s technology-driven world. One challenge I faced was organizing the information so that it remained simple and visually clear without adding too much text. At the same time, choosing suitable icons and arranging the sections creatively was very interesting. This task helped me understand how visual design can make educational information easier to read and remember.
